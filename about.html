Who We Are

The Brooklyn Technical High School Solar Car Team is a dedicated group of hard working, smart, and creative individuals who aspire to create their school's very first solar car.

Recently, the team met with our principal, David Newman, to discuss logistics with our fabulous alumni foundation as well as ground the club at our school as an official team at BTHS. There was nothing short of a positive response and the team works harder every day.
